ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Are you ready to take part in a national dialogue driving transformation in the agricultural sector?

The TARI Conference is a key platform that brings together researchers, farmers, development stakeholders, and policymakers to explore the future of agriculture in Tanzania. Organized by the Tanzania Agricultural Research Institute (TARI), the conference aims to promote innovation, knowledge sharing, and the development of modern solutions in agricultural production.

Throughout the conference, participants gain the opportunity to explore new research findings, learn about innovative agricultural technologies, and engage in meaningful discussions on challenges facing the sector — from climate change to improving productivity for smallholder farmers. It also provides a valuable space for professional networking and collaboration on impactful development initiatives.

In addition to discussions and presentations, the conference features exhibitions of agricultural products and technologies. These exhibitions allow participants to experience practical solutions that have the potential to transform farmers’ livelihoods and enhance productivity.

Held in the city of Dodoma, the conference also offers visitors a chance to experience the hospitality of Tanzania’s capital while contributing to the growth of a vital sector of the national economy.

TARI Conference — where research meets action for sustainable agricultural development.
